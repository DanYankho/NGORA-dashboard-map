NGO Distribution Map — Guide

Map Colours

  • Deeper blue = more NGOs registered in that district
  • Light blue = fewer NGOs; near-white = very few
  • Colour scale uses a square-root curve to show variation across smaller districts

District Interaction

  • Click any district to zoom in and see its NGO breakdown
  • Click again or press ESC to zoom out
  • Hold Shift + district abbreviation (e.g. Shift+LL for Lilongwe) to jump to that district

Panels

  • Top Districts (right) — Top 10 districts ranked by total NGO count
  • NGO Overview (left) — National totals: local vs international breakdown
  • District Detail (left, when selected) — That district's full NGO breakdown with national context
  • Use ↑ / ↓ arrows or click arrows inside panels to page through results
